Symbols and Meanings

Flags often contain various symbols and colors that have specific meanings:

  • Red: Courage and sacrifice.
  • Blue: Freedom and peace.
  • Green: Hope and fertility.
  • Yellow: Wealth and prosperity.

Each country's flag has its unique design, often reflecting its history, culture, and values.
